Welcome to LeadRev — the B2B revenue intelligence platform built for sales teams who can't afford to let deals slip through the cracks. LeadRev automatically analyzes your pipeline, flags overdue, untouched, and high-value risk leads, and surfaces a prioritized Recovery Queue so your team always knows exactly where to focus next. Whether you're managing a handful of enterprise accounts or thousands of leads across a large sales org, LeadRev gives you the visibility and tooling to stop revenue leakage before it happens.

## Getting Started

Follow these four steps to go from zero to a fully operational revenue recovery workflow inside LeadRev.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Import your leads">
    Bring your existing pipeline into LeadRev via CSV upload, CRM sync, or the REST API. Accurate data — including deal values, stages, and last contact dates — gives the risk engine everything it needs to make precise detections.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review risk detections">
    Once your leads are imported, LeadRev automatically runs its risk analysis engine. It identifies overdue deals, leads with no next step, untouched accounts, and high-value opportunities at risk of going cold.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Work the Recovery Queue">
    The Recovery Queue ranks every flagged lead by urgency and potential revenue impact. Work through the queue by logging calls, sending follow-up emails, creating tasks, and updating deal stages — all without leaving LeadRev.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track pipeline leakage">
    Monitor your Pipeline Health dashboard to see total revenue at risk, leakage trends over time, and how your team's recovery actions are moving the needle. Use these insights to coach reps and refine your sales process.
  </Step>
</Steps>
