LeadRev is a B2B revenue intelligence platform that gives sales teams continuous visibility into the health of their pipeline. In most sales organizations, leads fall through the cracks quietly — deals stall between stages, follow-ups are missed, and high-value accounts go untouched for weeks while reps focus elsewhere. By the time someone notices, the opportunity is cold or already lost to a competitor. LeadRev solves this by automatically scanning your pipeline for risk signals — overdue deals, missing next steps, uncontacted leads, and high-value accounts with no recent activity — and surfacing them in a prioritized Recovery Queue before revenue leaks out of your funnel.

## The LeadRev Workflow

LeadRev is designed around a clear, repeatable workflow that keeps your team proactive rather than reactive.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Import leads">
    Pull your pipeline into LeadRev from a CSV export, a connected CRM like Salesforce or HubSpot, or directly via the REST API. The more data you provide — deal values, stages, last contact dates, and next steps — the more precise the risk analysis becomes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Risk detection runs automatically">
    As soon as leads are imported or updated, LeadRev's risk engine evaluates each one against your configured rules. It flags overdue deals, leads with no next step scheduled, accounts that haven't been contacted within your defined thresholds, and high-value opportunities showing signs of stalling.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the Recovery Queue">
    The Recovery Queue surfaces every flagged lead, ranked by a combination of urgency, days since last contact, and deal value. Your team gets a clear, prioritized list of exactly which leads to focus on today — no manual triage required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log contact or create tasks">
    Take action directly from the Recovery Queue. Log a call, email, or meeting against any lead to record what happened and reset its risk status. Create follow-up tasks with due dates to make sure nothing slips again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor pipeline health">
    Track total revenue at risk, leakage trends, recovery rates, and team activity across your Pipeline Health dashboard. Use the data to coach reps, refine your sales process, and report on pipeline quality to leadership.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Capabilities

  <Card title="Lead Health Scoring" icon="heart-pulse" href="/concepts/lead-health">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Recovery Queue" icon="list-check" href="/concepts/recovery-queue">
    A continuously updated, prioritized list of your highest-risk leads. The queue ranks opportunities by urgency and potential revenue impact so your team always knows where to start.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Revenue Leakage Detection" icon="triangle-exclamation" href="/concepts/pipeline-risk">
    LeadRev quantifies the dollar value of at-risk pipeline in real time. See exactly how much revenue is sitting in stalled or neglected deals so you can make a business case for recovery action.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Contact & Task Logging" icon="clipboard-list" href="/guides/logging-contact">
    Log calls, emails, and meetings against any lead in seconds. Create tasks with due dates and assignees to ensure every deal has a clear next step and owner.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pipeline Dashboard" icon="chart-line" href="/guides/pipeline-dashboard">
    A real-time view of your pipeline health across stages, owners, and risk categories. Track recovery trends over time and measure the impact of your team's outreach efforts.

  <Card title="Configurable Risk Rules" icon="sliders" href="/configuration/risk-thresholds">

  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who It's For

LeadRev is built for sales managers, revenue operations teams, and account executives who own pipeline health and are accountable for hitting quota. If you're a sales manager looking to coach your team with data rather than gut feel, a RevOps leader who needs to report on pipeline quality and forecast accuracy, or an AE carrying a large book of business and struggling to stay on top of every deal, LeadRev gives you the visibility and workflows to operate at a higher level. It works best in teams where deals have a defined sales cycle and regular follow-up is critical to closing — making it especially valuable in mid-market and enterprise B2B sales environments.
