Revenue intelligence in LeadRev is the automated, continuous analysis of your lead pipeline designed to detect where deals are stalling, which leads have gone cold, and where revenue is silently leaking out of your funnel. Rather than relying on manual pipeline reviews or gut instinct, LeadRev evaluates a set of behavioral and structural signals across every lead — then translates those signals into health scores, risk flags, and a prioritized Recovery Queue your team can act on immediately.

## How LeadRev Analyzes Your Pipeline

LeadRev monitors five core signal types across every lead in your pipeline. Together, these signals give LeadRev a complete picture of where engagement is healthy and where deals are drifting toward loss.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Last Contact Recency" icon="clock">
    The number of days since your team last had a touchpoint with this lead — a call, email, meeting, or logged activity. The longer the gap, the higher the risk signal weight.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Next Step Presence" icon="circle-check">
    Whether a defined next step or task exists for this lead. A lead with no next step has no forward momentum — LeadRev treats its absence as an immediate risk indicator.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Deal Value at Risk" icon="dollar-sign">
    The monetary value associated with this lead or opportunity. Higher-value deals receive elevated risk priority so your team focuses recovery effort where the revenue impact is greatest.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Time in Current Stage" icon="chart-bar">
    How many days this lead has been sitting in its current pipeline stage without progressing. Extended stage age signals a stall, even if recent contact has occurred.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lead Age" icon="calendar">
    How long ago this lead was created or imported. A newly imported lead that has never been touched carries a different risk profile than an older lead with a rich history of engagement.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Untouched Since Import" icon="inbox">
    Whether your team has logged any activity at all since the lead entered LeadRev. Leads with zero recorded touchpoints surface immediately as high-priority recovery targets.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## From Signals to Actions

LeadRev turns raw pipeline signals into concrete, rep-ready actions through a consistent four-step evaluation loop that runs continuously in the background.

1. **Signal evaluation** — LeadRev monitors each of the signal types above across your full lead database, updating whenever your team logs activity or changes lead data.
2. **Health scoring** — Each lead receives a health score from 0–100 and a corresponding risk tier (Healthy, At Risk, or Critical) based on the combined weight of its signals.
3. **Recovery Queue population** — Leads that cross a risk threshold are automatically surfaced in the Recovery Queue, ranked by severity and deal value so your team always knows what to work first.
4. **Rep action and real-time updates** — When a rep logs a contact, completes a task, or updates lead metadata, LeadRev recalculates the health score immediately. The Recovery Queue re-ranks in real time to reflect the current state of your pipeline.

This loop means your pipeline intelligence is never stale — every action your team takes is reflected instantly across scores, queues, and dashboards.

## Why It Matters

Manual pipeline reviews — weekly one-on-ones, spreadsheet audits, CRM spot checks — are periodic by nature. Between reviews, deals drift, contacts go cold, and high-value opportunities silently age out of reach. By the time a manager notices a stalled deal, the window for recovery has often already closed.

LeadRev eliminates that blind spot entirely. Because signal evaluation runs continuously and health scores update in real time, your team surfaces at-risk deals the moment they start showing warning signs — not a week later during a pipeline call. The result is fewer deals lost to neglect, more consistent follow-through across your entire lead base, and a clear, prioritized action list every time a rep opens LeadRev.
